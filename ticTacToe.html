How to play
Tic Tac Toe is a pretty simple game to play: You start by clicking in one of the 9 squares above. When you have clicked in one of the squares, an "O" is placed in it. The goal is to try to get 3 "O"s in a row. (Either vertically, horizontally, or diagonally)
However, the computer can block you (you each take turns placing "markers" in the boxes, and the computer places "X"s instead of "O"s) and is trying to place three "X"s in a row. Whoever achieves this goal first wins. Good Luck!
