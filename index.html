How to be awesome:
  • Have good hygeine: Make sure to be civilized with people; you don't want people to know you by how dirty and unsanitary you are...
  • Have good eating habits: Don't eat just 'cause; you're going to get fat. Also, make sure to eat with your mouth closed, not talk while eating, eat with cutlery (when needed) and always be polite.
  • Don't be obnoxious: Being obnoxious, ignorant and loud is not awesome. Try to not make big reactions to things, it makes everyone feel awkward, and creates a bad impression.
  • Earn money: Having your parents buy everything for you is not cool. Besides, if you earn your own money, you'll learn how to manage your own money earlier on.
  • Speak properly and have good grammar: Try not to use slang words and use shortcuts on chats. Seriously, it's as if you never went to school!
  • Use big words: Expand your brain thesaurus, it won't only make you stand out and look smart, but it will help you later in life.
  • Don't follow the crowd: Don't be a follower, follow your ideas and make up your own trends. It will make you stand out from the crowd.
  • Don't brag and smother people with your own interests: The world isn't only about you, and the more you keep bragging and talking about your interest, the more annoying you will become.
  • Try new things that will be unique to you: The more unique aspects and skills you have, the more awesome you'll get!
  • Learn and ponder about the world: The more you know about anything, the more knowledge you'll have, which is when you truly become awesome...
How to be smart:
  • Ration your time: Don't waste all you time on social media or video games. Make sure to leave time (apart from your homework) to learn new stuff.
  • Connect what you learn to real life: That way, you'll not only know more about life, but you'll remember stuff you read or learn much more easily.
  • Play board and mind games instead of video games: Although video games might improve hand eye coordination, board games will do much more; they'll increase your intelligence.
  • Create a goal of stuff you want to learn more about: Like this, you have a goal to shoot for, and in accomplishing it, you'll learn a whole lot more about the world!
  • Have smart friends: Although this sounds stupid, having smart friends means that you can learn from them as well as compete with them.
  • Learn a new language: Apart from being able to brag that you know a lot of languages, learning a new language also helps form new connections in the brain.
  • Explain stuff to others: "If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it well enough," Albert Einstein said. By explaining topics to other people, you strengthen your memory on that topic.
  • Explore a topic in depth: No only will you learn a lot about that particular topic, but along the way, you can also end up learning about a whole variety of other topics!
  • Read a lot: Yes, yes, this is very clich√©, but it works! The more you read, the more knowledge and connections you'll have!
  • Challenge yourself: When you challenge youreslf, you explore new ideas and opinions you never thought were possible. So the next time you have an idea or project to do, go the extra mile to complete it! Good luck!
